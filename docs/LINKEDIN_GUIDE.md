## 💼 Interview Talking Points

### "Tell me about a project you're proud of"

"I'm particularly proud of my customer churn prediction project. I analyzed 5,000 e-commerce customers to build a predictive model that identifies at-risk customers before they churn.

What made this interesting was combining machine learning with business strategy. The model achieved 99% accuracy, but more importantly, it revealed that website visit frequency was the strongest predictor - something the business could act on immediately.

I used RFM segmentation to create customer profiles, which showed that our 'At Risk' segment - about 15% of customers - represented 30% of potential revenue loss. This allowed the business to prioritize retention efforts.

The project demonstrates my ability to work with data end-to-end: from data cleaning and EDA through machine learning to generating actionable business recommendations. It's all documented on my GitHub with reproducible code."

### "What was your biggest challenge?"

"The biggest challenge was actually not technical - it was understanding what insights would be most valuable to the business.

Initially, I focused on maximizing model accuracy. But in conversations with stakeholders, I realized they needed to understand *why* customers were churning and *what* to do about it, not just get predictions.

This led me to focus more on feature importance and customer segmentation. I created customer profiles like 'Champions' vs 'At Risk' vs 'Lost' that were more intuitive than just raw predictions.

I also had to balance model complexity with interpretability. While I could have used deep learning, the Random Forest was more transparent and easier to explain to non-technical stakeholders."
